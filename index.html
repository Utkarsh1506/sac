Our Teams

Technical Affairs

Welcome to the Technical Affairs section of the Student Affairs Council. Our team is dedicated to fostering technical excellence among students, providing resources and support for innovation, and organizing events that enhance technical skills.

Hostel Affairs

Welcome to Hostel Affairs – your go-to place for all matters related to campus living. We are here to ensure a comfortable and enriching experience for residents in the university hostels.

Academic Affairs

Navigate your academic journey with the support of the Academic Affairs team. We are committed to enhancing the educational experience for all students.

Sports Affairs

The Sports Department of the Student Affairs Council is here to ignite your passion for fitness and competition. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, we have something for everyone.

Design and Media Team

Welcome to the Design and Media Team, where creativity meets communication. Our team is dedicated to visually enhancing the student experience and promoting council initiatives.

Event Management Team

Leading the charge, they set the vision, manage the budget, and coordinate the entire operation. Think baton-wielding maestro, ensuring every section plays in harmony.

Day Scholars Affairs

Day scholars, this section is designed with you in mind. Discover resources and support tailored to your unique needs as a student living off-campus.

Cultural Affairs

Celebrate diversity and cultural richness with the Cultural Affairs team. Our mission is to create an inclusive campus environment where students from all backgrounds feel valued and respected.

ECell

Embark on your entrepreneurial journey with the E-Cell. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship among students.
